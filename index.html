President's Column

Heyy,

Hii this is me Nitya, yet introducing myself again. I’m glad that I’m back to writing all over it reminds me of my initial days in SHEILD. I’ve been a part of this beautiful organisation for more than a year now and for the changed person I think I am now SHEILD has played a major. I expect all the new recruiters of this semester to actually take me as an example to learn and grow in this organisation, if a person like me with low knowledge about startups could come this far by learning ,you guys can go way ahead than me. It’s not going to be easy but learning, mistakes, clash of ideas, arguments, falling apart, failures, success are all going to be a part of this journey for you all this year. Take this an opportunity to find out who you really are, where your interest lies and what you’re good at. SHEILD has been a very successful organisation in any many aspects and has given very good events for the fests. Hopefully SHEILD will continue to conduct successful events, help the startups that will emerge in VIT and create efficient entrepreneurs.

Goals completed this month.

  • The first thing would be this very newsletter which all of you are reading is being implemented finally.
  • The content for the website by the editorial department.
  • A beautiful event called ideathon has been made for the upcoming fest (BIDSF) by the events department.
  • A feasible and budget friendly plan for the event ideathon has been made by the finance department.
  • The university relations department has done an amazing job in getting permissions for the desks in TT and outside DC and also succeeded in getting night slips.
  • The PR department has managed to get three mentors for the upcoming event ideathon and is working on getting a couple of more mentors for the event.
  • The PR dept has also gotten in touch with the mentors of the previous event who want to come back for an upcoming event in Gravitas.
  • We finally have an Instagram account for SHEILD and it has been possible by the marketing department.
  • Two very good articles on the in SHEILD VIT page which were informative.
  • Marketing department has been working on increasing the reach of the Facebook page by looking for page mergers.
  • Have been working on the marketing strategies of the upcoming event.
  • The reach of the Facebook page upto 1,162 likes.
  • The Startups department has announced its members.
  • HR has conducted a bonding session.
  • HR has been working member development and tracking members.
  • Making very attractive posters for the event which are being posted on the Facebook page.
  • 90% of the website has been completed and shall be launched in the next two weeks.
